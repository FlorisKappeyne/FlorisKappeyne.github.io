April 14, 2017

AGR: The Fastest Drifter

This video cannot be played. The project AGR: The Fastest Drifter is an Anti-Gravity Racing game, made with the custom cross-platform (Windows and PS4) Voodoo Engine. Created by a team of 6 programmers at first (the first block of 8 weeks), and 19 people (7 programmers, 12 designers and artists) at the end, it is one of the biggest projects I have worked on so far. In the end, it featured a fully functioning racing game for both single player and local multiplayer, audio, particle effects, several post processing effects on top of the physically based rendering, a complete game-flow with UI and intro cinematics and a suite of tools usable by designers to tweak the game while playing it.
