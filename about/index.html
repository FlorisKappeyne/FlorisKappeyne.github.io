About me

Floris Kappeyne van de Coppello

Hi, I’m Floris, and I like to play and program games. I made my first Hello World program when I was 15 years old for a school assignment. Since then, I’ve made a bunch of games and a few non-game related projects as well.

I’ve worked on several projects ranging from solo to 36 people, and anything in between. I have experience with a number of engines, including Unreal Engine 4, Unity and multiple custom engines (AGR: The Fastest Drifter for example).

Besides work, I enjoy talking about my latest project/game around the coffee machine. When I’m at home I like to work on my path tracer, play adventure games and watching Netflix with my girlfriend.

I usually program in C++ and am very comfortable with it. I also did some C# for Unity, most notably using it to create custom physics.
